About Blade & Fade

Where Style Meets Precision Welcome to Blade & Fade, Staines' premier destination for modern and classic barbering. At our barbershop, we combine timeless techniques with contemporary trends to deliver cuts, fades, and grooming services that make you look sharp and feel confident.

Our Story

Established with a passion for precision and style, Blade & Fade is more than just a barbershop—it's a place where community, craft, and creativity come together. From clean fades to traditional hot towel shaves, every service reflects our commitment to quality and attention to detail. We’ve built our reputation on two principles: exceptional barbering and unmatched client experience. Whether you're after a fresh look for the season, a regular tidy-up, or a relaxing grooming session, Blade & Fade is your trusted partner.

Why Choose Us?

Skilled Barbers: Our talented barbers are experts in their craft, blending traditional methods with the latest trends. Quality Service: We provide a welcoming, friendly atmosphere where every client gets personalized care. Community Vibe: Located in the heart of Staines, we pride ourselves on being a local favorite and a space where people can relax, connect, and leave looking their best.

Our Services

From sharp skin fades and detailed beard trims to classic cuts and luxury shaves, our services are designed to suit all styles and ages. We cater to modern trends while staying true to the timeless art of barbering.
